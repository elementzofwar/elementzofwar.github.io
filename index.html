Elementz of War

Glory Shall Be Ours!

Member Requirements

  • All Members Shall Be Friendly and Active
  • All Members need at Least 5 Donations to Be Considered Active
  • All Members need at Least 3 Crowns to be Considered Active
  • All Members Shall Request and Donate when Possible
  • All Members are Considered Present when they are a memorable member of the Clan

Elder Requirements

  • All Elders need to be Considered Active
  • All Elders need at Least 500 Three Crown Wins on there Profile
  • All Elders need at Least 1000 Wins on their Profile
  • All Elders need at Least 5000 Donations on there Profile
  • All Elders need to have been Considered Present
  • All Elders need to have been a part of the Clan for at Least 4 Weeks
  • An Member must have a Total of 500 Week Donations to be Considered for an Elder Position

Co-Leader Requirements

  • All Co-Leaders need to be Considered Active
  • All Co-Leaders need to be Considered Present
  • All Co-Leaders need to be Considered an Elder
  • An Elder must have a Total of 800 Week Donations to be Considered for a Co-Leader Position

Demotions

  • All Members of Any Rank that is not Active will be Demoted to the Lower Rank after a Specific Amount of Time
  • All Members of Any Rank that Reach Below the Trophy Threshold Specified on the Clan Description will also be Demoted
  • Any Member that cannot be Demoted will be Kicked
  • Any Demotions or Kicks must be Announced a Day Before
  • Exceptions: Any IRL(In Real Life) Events that Take Priority of Clash Royale that cause a Player to be not Active will be not Demoted or Kicked but Notify Members of Rank As Soon As Possible
  • Note: All Ranked Members Must Notify Other Ranked Members of Any Exceptions that may Occur for Other Potential Members

Clan Composition

  • Total Members: 50
  • Total Co-Leaders: 5
  • Total Elders: 10 - 20
  • Total Members: 50

Please Request for a Promotion Once You Have Reached Passed the Requirements. Before the Weekly Reset.
